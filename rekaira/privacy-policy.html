Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2025

At Reka Ira, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website rekaira.lk.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information in the following ways:

a. Contact or Project Forms

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Project idea or message content

b. Career Applications

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Portfolio link
  • Uploaded CV and Cover Letter

c. Analytics

  • Anonymous browsing data (via Google Analytics)
  • Location, browser, device type, and session duration

We do not collect passwords or sensitive payment data via this website.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to inquiries and project submissions
  • Evaluate and process job applications
  • Improve website functionality and visitor experience
  • Analyze site traffic and engagement

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Retention

  • Job application data may be retained for up to 12 months for future openings
  • Contact form submissions are stored securely and reviewed periodically
  • Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics policy (default: 14 months)

4. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over secure (HTTPS) connections
  • CVs and messages submitted via forms are stored securely and accessed only by authorized staff

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for analytics and basic performance tracking. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser.

6. Your Rights

If you are located in the EU or a jurisdiction with similar protections (e.g., GDPR):

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data
  • You may opt-out of analytics tracking (via browser plugins or cookie settings)

To exercise these rights, email us at: hello@rekaira.com

7. International Visitors

By using this site, you agree that your data may be processed and stored in Sri Lanka, even if you are located outside of Sri Lanka.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: rekaira@gmail.com
Address: Karapitiya, Galle, Sri Lanka.
